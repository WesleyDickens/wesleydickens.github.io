What This Website Is And Isn't The Dark Web - Explained

The first thing you probably noticed about this website is that it's ugly. This both is and isn't on purpose.

Now that my company is implementing a four day work week, I wanted to take this as an opportunity to grow both personally and professionally. I plan to accomplish this by learning for the sake of learning, and the best way for me to do that is by explaining things.

The true mark of understand something is your ability to explain it to someone else who has no prior knowledge. So when I find something I don't understand, my goal is to research and learn as much as I can and then write a post explaining it.

Now, why is my website ugly?

I started out building something on Squarespace and it was really easy. Too easy. I came to the conclusion that if this whole Friday project is about growing and learning, I should take this opportunity to learn something new -- How to build a website.

I haven't touched HTML or CSS since learning how to change the cursor on my Myspace page; And I haven't used Javascript since I built a phishing website to steal Runescape accounts in middle school. (for legal reasons that's a joke)

All that to say, I have a lot of learning to do before this website looks nice, and I couldn't be more excited about that.

So, what is this website/blog meant to be?

This blog is going to be a brain dump of things I'm curious about -- There is no theme beyond that.

So, what is this website/blog not meant to be?

I have no plans to monetize this, or advertise it in any way. If you're reading this, then odds are I sent it to you personally.

I also don't want this to serve as a personal diary, I have my own private journal for that. So I won't be writing about anything personal or opinionated.

That's it, thanks for stopping by!
